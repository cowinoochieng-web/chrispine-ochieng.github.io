About Me

The person who keeps things running.

I am a cross-functional Operations and IT professional at WeTu / We!Hub Victoria Ltd., a clean energy and social enterprise serving fishing and farming communities in western Kenya. Over six years I have grown from Hub Operator to the organisation's single cross-departmental operations and IT resource — embedded across every business unit, trusted to make decisions and maintain continuity when it matters most.

My work spans solar energy, cold chain logistics, water infrastructure, e-mobility, digital product development, and circular economy programmes. I am equally comfortable troubleshooting a solar system at a remote hub, leading a software development sprint, or training a team on a new SOP.

I am currently pursuing a BSc in Computer Science at the Open University of Kenya, building on a Diploma in Software Engineering from Moringa School — and I bring that technical depth directly into the operations I manage every day.
